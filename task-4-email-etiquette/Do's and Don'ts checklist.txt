Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (College Students)
✅ Do’s
Think before posting — consider how your content may be perceived by others.
Protect your privacy — adjust privacy settings and limit personal information shared.
Maintain professionalism — remember future employers may view your profile.
Verify information before sharing — avoid spreading misinformation.
Engage respectfully — be polite and constructive in comments and discussions.
❌ Don’ts
Don’t share sensitive information — such as your address, phone number, or passwords.
Don’t post inappropriate content — avoid offensive, harmful, or illegal material.
Don’t engage in online arguments or harassment — it can harm your reputation.
Don’t overshare personal life details — maintain healthy boundaries online.
Don’t ignore your digital footprint — everything you post can have long-term consequences.
